Skattefil — file your Swedish tax return from your phone.

Identify yourself securely with BankID, review your pre-filled declaration, and submit it to Skatteverket in minutes. Your identity is verified through BankID, Sweden's national electronic identification system — no separate account or password to manage.

Features:
- Log in with BankID (mobile or on this device)
- Review and adjust your pre-filled income figures
- Submit your declaration and get a digital receipt
- Reminders before the filing deadline
